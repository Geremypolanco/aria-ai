incorporation and no funding requirement** — the self-serve track is open to early,
bootstrapped startups with a website. Larger tiers ($10k–$100k) need an accelerator/VC
partner referral, so target the **Founders** tier.

- Apply at: https://aws.amazon.com/activate/
