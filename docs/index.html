FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this just another AI chatbot?

No. ARIA acts on its own — it runs strategies, publishes, and processes payments without you prompting it. A chatbot waits; ARIA works.

What happens to my credentials?

You connect the accounts you want ARIA to use (e.g. Stripe, social, email). They're stored as secrets, used only to act on your behalf, and you can revoke them anytime.

Where does it run, and can I pause it?

ARIA runs in the cloud, always on. You can pause or cancel in one click — nothing is locked in.

What does "pay only once it works" mean?

For the audit and the done-for-you build, you don't pay until the first automation is live and producing for you. The risk is on us.
